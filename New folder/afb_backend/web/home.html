Who We Are

From roasting room to route truck.

American Food & Beverage — operating under our Grassland and Premium Food lines — imports, roasts, flavors, and packs dried fruit, nuts, trail mixes, granola, candy, and chocolate-covered items for grocery, foodservice, and independent retailers across the Tri-State area.

We source directly from growers, processors, and packers worldwide, then bring it all together under one roof in Paterson, NJ — including our own custom packaging and private-label programs.

  • Roasting & flavoring — done in-house, not outsourced
  • Private label — custom packaging & branding available
  • Delivery — our own fleet, next day across the Tri-State
  • Sourcing — direct relationships with growers & packers worldwide
